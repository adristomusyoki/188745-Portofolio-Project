My Projects

  • Project One – IS PROJECT
    Information Systems for Student Club Management
    Developed a web-based application to streamline club membership, event scheduling, and communication for university student organizations. The system allows club leaders to manage member data, send announcements, and track event attendance efficiently.
    Technologies Used: PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
    Impact: Improved club operations and member engagement at Strathmore University, reducing manual paperwork and enhancing collaboration.
  • Project Two – VW GOLF R MK 7.5 PROJECT
    Automotive Content Creation & Community Engagement
    Produced a series of video documentaries and blog posts showcasing the build, customization, and performance upgrades of a VW Golf R MK 7.5. The project involved collaborating with local car enthusiasts, mechanics, and brands to highlight the Kenyan car scene.
    Technologies Used: Adobe Premiere Pro, Photoshop, DSLR Photography, WordPress
    Impact: Fostered a vibrant online community, inspired automotive enthusiasts, and promoted safe, creative car culture in Kenya.
